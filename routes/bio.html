Bio

Aaron Pietsch

A Professional Journey

Aaron's journey as a developer began in college, while working at Bank of America.

He taught himself VBA and ended up streamlining his job. Afterwards, he found himself with a lot of free time on his hands and basically eliminated the need for his own role!

Upon discovering this fascination with the power of automation and basic programming, he joined a web-development start-up called Jurispage

While still taking college classes, Aaron worked full-time at Jurispage for a year. Jurispage designs websites for small law firms and manages online marketing for them

As the sole project manager, Aaron worked as liaison between the salesperson, developer, client, and content writers to push out polished finished websites

Over a year, Jurispage grew exponentially and was sold to a larger company for seven figures.

At the time of the sale, Aaron was graduating and had saved some money. He traveled for that summer and traveled. What was supposed to be a month turned into 2, and then 3. In the end, Aaron settled in Vietnam for 7 months where he became an English teacher.

After getting serious about this idea, Aaron returned to the U.S. where he did a deep-dive into learning full-stack development, absorbing everything available and creating personal projects

Aaron is now a full-fledged developer with professional project management experience.
